About

Who we are

The Drexel Algorithms and Data Structures student organization is a group of students interested in developing their programming skills and honing their algorithmic intuition through regular training, simulations, and coding competitions.

  • Help students become better programmers by exposing them to many different algorithms and data structure use cases
  • Sharing experiences from different programming competitions
  • Keeping our eyes on the end goal: passing the technical interview

Our members have built a rapport with several Big-N recruiters at companies like Facebook, Amazon, Waymo, and Twitter. Though these companies are very secretive about their specific interview practices, DrexelADS can prepare you for anything they might throw at you by giving you a depth and breadth of knowledge in all the most common algorithms you may need to succeed.
